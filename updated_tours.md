# Culture & Heritage Tours
### 01\. Ancient Wonders Escape | 7 Days / 6 Nights
·       SEO Meta Description: Discover Sri Lanka’s ancient culture and heritage on a 7-day immersive tour.
 

The Ancient Wonders Escape tour of Sri Lanka offers a journey through the island’s most iconic cultural and historical landmarks, where ancient kingdoms and sacred temples tell the story of early Sinhalese civilization. This tour connects some of Sri Lanka’s most fascinating heritage sites, blending UNESCO World Heritage sites, royal cities, and cultural experiences into a 7-day itinerary. Travelers can explore ancient ruins, climb iconic rock fortresses, visit cave temples, and experience the living traditions of Sri Lanka.
On the Ancient Wonders Escape tour, we bring together historic towns and cultural highlights in the island’s heart, offering insights into Sri Lanka’s rich history, vibrant culture, and architectural marvels. Here’s your guide to the must-see experiences, along with tips from our team to make the journey unforgettable.
 

**Tour Overview – Travel Itinerary – Journey Highlights – Quick Enquiry**
### Tour Overview
·       Destinations: 
Negombo – Dambulla – Sigiriya – Polonnaruwa – Kandy – Colombo  
·       Durations: 6 Nights / 7 Days
·       Tour Type: Culture & Heritage
·       Ideal For: First-time visitors, history lovers, relaxed cultural travel
 

### Travel Itinerary
·       Day 01 | BIA to Negombo 
Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport and meet your chauffeur guide. Transfer to Negombo to relax after your flight. Enjoy the evening at leisure, or take a short walk along the beach.
·       Day 02 | Negombo to Dambulla
Travel to Dambulla and explore the Dambulla Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its ancient Buddhist murals and statues. Evening at leisure.
·       Day 03 | Dambulla to Sigiriya
Climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, marvel at its frescoes, Mirror Wall, and Lion’s Paw. Optionally, visit a traditional village to experience authentic Sri Lankan life and cuisine.
·       Day 04 | Sigiriya to Polonnaruwa
Visit the ancient city of Polonnaruwa, the medieval capital of Sri Lanka. Explore the Royal Palace, Gal Vihara, and the Sacred Quadrangle, immersing yourself in the island’s rich history.
·       Day 05 | Polonnaruwa to Kandy
Drive to Kandy, stopping at a spice garden along the way. In the evening, enjoy a captivating Kandy Cultural Dance Show, showcasing traditional music and dance.
 

·       Day 06 | Kandy to Colombo
Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, stroll around Kandy Lake, and explore local markets and the gem museum. Free time for shopping or relaxation in the afternoon.
·       Day 07 | Colombo to BIA
Transfer to Colombo for an optional city tour and shopping before being dropped at the airport or your hotel for departure.

·       Day 08 | Kandy to Colombo
After breakfast, travel to Colombo. Enjoy an optional city tour including colonial landmarks, shopping areas, and modern attractions. Free time to relax in the evening.

·       Kandy – Lakeside Romance: Enjoy peaceful moments beside the scenic lake and green surroundings.
·       Nuwara Eliya – Tea Country Love: Wander through misty tea estates and cool-climate gardens.
·       Scenic Hill Country Train Ride: Share one of the world’s most romantic train journeys together.
·       Ella – Mountain Views & Sunrises: Experience breathtaking viewpoints and tranquil hill town vibes.
·       Cool Climate Escape: Enjoy refreshing mountain air and cozy evenings.
·       ala National Park – Safari Adventure: Spot leopards, elephants, and wildlife on an exciting jeep safari.
·       South Coast – Beach & Relaxation: Enjoy private time on golden beaches.
·       Galle Dutch Fort (UNESCO): Stroll through charming colonial streets and historic sites.
·       Private Couple Time: A perfectly paced itinerary designed for romance and relaxation.
·       Colombo – Leisure & Shopping: End your getaway with relaxed city time before departure.
